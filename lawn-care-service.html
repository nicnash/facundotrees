Our Most-Requested Lawn Care Services

Sod Installation

Creates an instant, weed-free lawn by laying premium sod, ensuring strong root contact, proper grading, and first-month care for a lush, even yard ready for foot traffic fast.

Mulching

Spreads nutrient-rich hardwood or pine-bark mulch to lock in moisture, suppress weeds, regulate soil temperature, and give landscape beds a crisp, finished look.

Hedge Trimming

Shapes hedges and ornamental shrubs with clean cuts that promote dense growth, maintain desired height, and keep foundation plantings neat all season long.

Our fence-line clearing service cuts vegetation flush to the soil, removes invasive roots, and hauls away all debris, leaving a clean, walkable strip that protects your fence.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does sod installation cost?

Most Houston yards run $1.25–$1.75 per sq ft, including removal of old turf, laser grading, fresh St. Augustine or Bermuda, and power-rolling. We measure on site and give a fixed, line-item quote.

What type of sod is best for my yard?

St. Augustine handles shade and heavy foot traffic, while Bermuda thrives in full-sun, high-drainage areas. We'll test your soil and exposure before recommending a cultivar.

Can you level a yard that holds water after rain?

Yes—our skid-steer box blade and laser transit reshape low spots, add topsoil, and set a 1–2% slope away from the house so stormwater drains properly before sod goes down.

How quickly can you start?

Most lawn care jobs are booked within 24–48 hours. For urgent needs like pre-sale cleanup or storm prep, we can often accommodate same-day work.
