Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We answer calls around the clock. During single-home emergencies, crews are typically on-site within hours. During city-wide storm events we triage by severity and give you a clear ETA.

Will my homeowner's insurance cover emergency tree removal?

Coverage varies by policy. Typically, if the tree damages a covered structure, removal costs are partially covered. We provide detailed photos, invoices, and written reports to support your claim.

What if the tree is on the property line?

Texas law generally makes each property owner responsible for damage on their own side. We walk both parties through fair, documented next steps to avoid disputes.

Can you tarp a damaged roof the same visit?

Yes. We carry tarps and plywood and can install temporary roof protection immediately after clearing the tree, keeping rain out until professional roofers arrive.

Are you insured for large crane work?

Absolutely. We carry $2 million in general liability and have a state-licensed crane operator on every large removal. Proof of coverage is emailed on request.
